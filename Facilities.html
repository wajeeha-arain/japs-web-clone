Jeejal Amaan Public School's Facilities

ACTIVITY ROOMS

Our vibrant Activity Rooms designed for preschool students.
These enchanting spaces are filled with colors, toys, and interactive games that
nurture young minds, fostering creativity, social skills, and a love for learning through play.

COMPUTER LAB
PROF. DR. AJMAL SAWAND (SHAHEED)
Welcome to the gateway of digital exploratio n our Computer Lab. This state-of-the-art facility empowers students to delve into the realm of technology. From coding to research, witness how this lab nurtures essential skills for a tech-savvy future. This Lab is dedicated to the renowned professor of artificial intelligence Shaheed Ajmal Sawand. This lab was inaugurated by Prof. Dr. Engr. Zahid Hussain Khand. The lab is equipped with 30 computer systems with the latest technology and required accessories.

SCIENCE LAB
Embark on a journey of scientific discovery in our cutting edge Science Lab.
Equipped with state of the art tools and resources, students delve into hands-on
experiments, fostering curiosity and critical thinking. Explore how this dynamic space
ignites a passion for exploration and learning. The lab is equipped with the
required facilities to perform practical science experiments and
demonstrate the hand on experience of theoretical knowledge & knowledge of astronomy
through latest telescopic & radio dishes.

SOFI SHAH INAYAT SHAHEED LIBRARY
Here, students embark on literary journeys, exploring books that expand horizons and spark creativity.
Whether it’s for research or leisure, the library is your gateway to endless learning and adventure.

AUDITORIUM

AUDITORIUM !
Within the building’s embrace, a 100-person auditorium awakens with the promise of diverse engagement. Sun-drenched and spacious, it boasts plush, reclining chairs arranged for optimal comfort and focus.

A cutting-edge sound system amplifies every thought and performance, fostering lectures, seminars, and training sessions that spark intellectual fires. But this space transcends mere academia.

With flexible seating and a welcoming aura, it transforms to host movie nights, musical soirees, or even informal gatherings. In its very essence, the auditorium reflects the boundless creativity it nurtures, a vibrant stage for the exchange of ideas and the celebration of the human spirit.
